Dedicated to Bone and Joint Health

Sri Krishna Orthopedic Hospital is a premier 25-bed specialty care center situated in the heart of Gajuwaka, Visakhapatnam. Guided by clinical excellence and ethical practices, we provide state-of-the-art diagnostic and surgical solutions for complex bone and joint disorders.

Under the visionary leadership of Dr. M. Murali Mohan (MS, Ortho), our hospital has earned the trust of thousands of patients across Andhra Pradesh. We combine cutting-edge technology, modern modular operation theaters, and specialized rehabilitation to ensure rapid, painless recovery for our patients.

Our Mission

To deliver advanced, high-quality orthopedic care with empathy, safety, and transparency, restoring patients to active, pain-free lives.

To be the most trusted center of excellence for orthopedic surgery and physical rehabilitation in the region, recognized for positive patient outcomes.

Joint Replacement

  • Rapid Recovery Protocols
  • High-Flexion Implants

Spine Surgery

Advanced treatment for disc herniations, spinal stenosis, sciatica, and corrective spine stabilization using computer-assisted planning.

  • Microdiscectomy
  • Spinal Decompression
  • Sciatica Management

Trauma & Fracture Care

Round-the-clock polytrauma management, complex fracture fixation, non-union corrections, and emergency bone stabilization.

  • 24/7 Emergency Care
  • Intramedullary Nailing
  • Locking Plate Fixation

Arthroscopy

Keyhole surgeries for diagnosing and repairing internal joint structures, ensuring minimal scarring and faster recovery.

  • Knee & Shoulder Arthroscopy
  • Meniscus Repair
  • Diagnostic Joint Inspection

Sports Medicine

Treatment of ligament tears (ACL/PCL reconstruction), tendon injuries, joint instability, and tailored athletic recovery plans.

  • ACL/PCL Reconstruction
  • Rotator Cuff Repair
  • Athletic Conditioning

Pediatric Orthopedics

Corrective treatment for congenital bone deformities (clubfoot, hip dysplasia), pediatric fractures, and childhood bone disorders.

  • Clubfoot Correction
  • Deformity Stabilization
  • Pediatric Trauma Care

Our Facility

Advanced Medical Infrastructure

Modular Operating Theatre

Our state-of-the-art operating theater features laminar airflow systems, HEPA filtration, and clean-room wall paneling to reduce airborne infection risks to near zero. Equipped with modern anesthetic systems and advanced orthopedic navigation tools.

Digital X-Ray & Diagnostics

Fitted with high-frequency digital radiograph equipment that delivers sharp, micro-detailed images within seconds. This allows our orthopedic team to assess joint alignment, fracture severity, and bone densities quickly and initiate treatment instantly.

Physiotherapy & Rehab Unit

A modern recovery facility loaded with advanced electrotherapy equipment, mechanical traction, muscle stimulators, and specialized exercise bars. Handled by expert therapists to accelerate rehabilitation post-surgery.

Most patients start standing or walking short distances with support within 24 hours after surgery. Normal daily activities can usually be resumed in 4 to 6 weeks. A dedicated physiotherapy protocol of 2 to 3 weeks at our rehab center is crucial to achieving optimal joint extension and strength.

Physiotherapy helps restore complete range of motion, muscle strength, and flexibility after bone healing. While the bone union happens naturally, immobilization causes muscle stiffness. Guided physical therapy ensures your joint regains full functional utility.

Professional Biography

Dr. M. Murali Mohan is a highly renowned orthopedic surgeon based in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. He founded Sri Krishna Orthopedic Hospital in 2011 with the goal of creating a dedicated center of excellence for bone, joint, and trauma care. He has successfully treated thousands of patients, restoring mobility to those suffering from degenerative joint conditions and complex physical trauma.

Area of Specializations

  • Primary & Revision Joint Replacement: Expertise in Total Knee Replacement (TKR) and Total Hip Replacement (THR) utilizing advanced low-wear implants.
  • Sports Medicine & Ligament Reconstruction: Arthroscopic repair of ACL, PCL, and meniscus tears, minimizing patient recovery duration.
  • Trauma & Complex Fractures: Experts in locked plating, intramedullary nailing, non-union corrections, and pediatric bone injuries.
  • Spine Care & Scientific Decompression: Handling chronic sciatica, cervical spondylosis, and spinal stability protocols.
