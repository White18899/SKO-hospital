A Cozy Local Nursing Home

Humble Service, Reliable Care

Established in 2011, Sri Krishna Orthopaedic Hospital is a small, compact, and patient-friendly 10-bed specialized nursing center in Gajuwaka. We focus on providing highly individual, reliable orthopaedic consulting and fracture recovery care without the heavy queues and corporate overhead of large hospitals.

We pride ourselves on clinical honesty. Every patient receives direct, one-on-one attention from our chief specialist, Dr. M. Murali Mohan. Whether it is casting a sudden fracture, treating a child's sprain, or managing severe joint pain for an elderly patient, our focus is on gentle, safe recovery.

Essential Medical Infrastructure

General Consult & Plaster Room
